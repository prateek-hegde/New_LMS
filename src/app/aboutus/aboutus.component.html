About Us



Business Toys is YOUR employer branding consultant for enhancing your brand visibility, brand engagement and brand equity across campuses in India through enriching digital and(or) on-ground campaigns in form of competitions.

For Companies and Institutes, we aim to conceptualise, market and execute campaigns in form of competitions, quizzes, hackathons, business strategy simulation games or any other gamified solution in a creative way to enable new dimensions of employer branding in campuses and competition-led-recruitment of future leaders.

For Students, we aim to provide timely, authentic and relevant information about all the critical elements of a student journey under one single platform - Competitions, Quizzes, Hackathons, Business Simulations, Scholarships, Internships, Recruiter Diaries & Insights etc.
